⛳️ Par2 — Short Game Tracker

Focused, smarter practice - tracked with sessions & data.

  • Every hole is a par 2
  • Start with a chip from a set distance and lie
  • Track how far you leave it from the hole, then putt out
  • The app logs your score, distance control, and dispersion
  • Use a rangefinder or step off the yardages
  • (1 stride or Pace = 3 feet)
